MUSIC THERAPY

l

Music Therapy is a practice where patients can play and listen to music to treat many mental health issues, such as stress, depression, or anxiety. Music therapy has been performed in many different enviornment during its years; It can be played in schools, psychiatric hospitals, outpatient clinics, senior centers, and much more! Music therapy can also help people cope with physical ailments such as chronic pain and hypertension! It has been shown to improve the conditions in the people who the practice is done with. Music therapy is used as an addition to regular treatments, and has been shown to improve depression when combined with medication or psychotherapy, even when compared with just standard treatments! Because of the interactivity of Music Therapy, clients will often play instruments or write music with their therapist. This allows for free self-expression, both individuall and communally with their therapist. Consider adding Music Therapy to your treatments, its success shows how Music improves mental health.
